Perspective

My perspective on AI and marketing.

The way customers find, evaluate, and choose brands is being rewritten. Search is no longer a list of links. It is an answer, generated in real time by a language model that decides which companies are worth mentioning.

The brands that win the next decade will not be the loudest. They will be the most clearly defined, the most consistently positioned, and the most legible to both human readers and machine reasoning. Marketing leadership is shifting from campaigns and channels to clarity, authority, and structured presence across the systems that now mediate discovery.

Frequently asked

Frequently asked questions.

Who is Joeri Billast?

Joeri Billast is a Belgian Fractional CMO, AI Visibility Strategist, and author of The Future CMO. He is based in Portugal and advises founders and executive teams on marketing leadership and brand positioning in AI-driven markets. He hosts the Web3 CMO Stories podcast and speaks internationally on the intersection of marketing and artificial intelligence.

What does a Fractional CMO do?

A Fractional CMO is a senior marketing leader who serves as Chief Marketing Officer for one or more companies on a part-time or interim basis. The role provides executive-level marketing strategy, leadership of internal marketing teams, and accountability for growth, without the cost of a full-time hire. Fractional CMOs are typically engaged by founder-led companies, scale-ups, and established businesses navigating a strategic transition.

What is AI Visibility Strategy?

AI Visibility Strategy is the practice of making a brand discoverable, accurately represented, and consistently recommended by AI systems such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google's generative search. It combines structured content, semantic clarity, authoritative positioning, and presence across the sources that large language models cite. As AI becomes the primary interface for discovery, AI visibility is becoming as foundational as SEO was for the previous era of the web.

What is The Future CMO?

The Future CMO is a book by Joeri Billast on the role of marketing leadership in an era reshaped by artificial intelligence. It examines how Chief Marketing Officers must adapt strategy, brand positioning, and decision-making to a market where AI mediates discovery, content, and customer experience. The book is endorsed by Philip Kotler.
